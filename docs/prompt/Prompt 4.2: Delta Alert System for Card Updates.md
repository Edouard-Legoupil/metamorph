Implement a delta alert system to monitor and alert on updates or contradictions affecting knowledge cards. The system must trigger alerts when new claims/triplets affect the data of any approved card, when contradictions or conflicts occur, when source documents are superseded, or as valid_until expiry approaches. Each alert should specify the card affected, trigger, relevant card sections, new facts/conflicts involved, severity, and status. Deliver alerts in the wiki/card UI and to curators/operators by banner, digest/email, and (where applicable) integrations like Slack/Teams. Provide options for the curator to update cards, acknowledge/dismiss/snooze alerts, or assign for renewal. Expiry logic should revert cards to draft and trigger the proper renewal process. Track analytics on alert frequency, time-to-resolution, and renewal cycles. Integrate all alerting into UI, operator dashboard, and agentic workflows.

Verification & Test Guidance
- [ ] Confirm a delta alert service is implemented with triggers as described, linking new facts, conflicts, and expiry to affected cards.
- [ ] Verify alert records are created with all required fields and surfaced in UI banners, notification lists, and dashboards.
- [ ] Email/slack/in-app/push integrations are wired up and can deliver at least one form of curator notification.
- [ ] Curator workflows exist for acknowledging, dismissing, snoozing, updating, or renewing cards based on alert status and expiry.
- [ ] Cards automatically revert to draft and become unavailable to agents/proposal tools at expiration/expiry.
- [ ] Analytics and operator dashboard/reporting show alert volumes, card updates, and time to review/renewal.
